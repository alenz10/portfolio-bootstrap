Cravings Magazine

For this project, I thought up several ideas for magazines, and did some research of current magazines. I chose one idea based on what I could easily get images of. I chose to make a baking magazine. Who doesn't love sweets? This magazine contains a table of contents, a department spread, three spreads for a feature article, an infographic, a sidebar, and a cover. We had to include out own images somewhere in the magazine or make graphics.
